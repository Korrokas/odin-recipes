Fruit Salad Recipe

Fruit salad is a delicious treat made up of nature's finest ingredients. There is no cooking involved here, just yummy diced up fruit ready to serve. The fruit chosen is up to each person, but this is what I believe to be the most delcious fruit salad!

Ingredients

  • Pineapple
  • Apple
  • Grapes
  • Kiwi
  • Blueberries
  • Strawberries

Steps

  1. Dice the pineapple into small to medium chunks
  2. Cut the strawberries in half
  3. Cut the kiwi into thin slices
  4. Cut the apple into bite sized chunks
  5. Mix all of the freshly cut fruit along with the blueberries and grapes into a serving bowl
  6. Enjoy!
