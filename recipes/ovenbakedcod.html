Oven Baked Cod Recipe

Delicious Homemade Cod

Seafood doesn't have to be a coastal delight enjoyed only on vacation! With some quality cod and some fresh ingredients, this fulfilling dinner can be enjoyed by everyone.

Ingredients

  • Cod (2 filets)
  • Lemon
  • Lime
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Prehead oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Cut botht the lemon and lime into slices
  3. Coat your baking pan with oil spray of your choice
  4. Coat both sides of the filets with garlic and onion powder for flavor
  5. Lay the slices of lemon and lime around the pan for the cod to absorb the flavor
  6. Bake for approximately 30-45 minutes until an internal temp of 145 F is reached
